THE PROCESS...

- After your purchase you will receive a confirmation email. Please reply to this email with the image you'd like to use. If you have a few options and are unsure which one to use, you can send them all to me and I will suggest which one I think will work best for an illustration. - For best results please ensure the photo you select is good quality, has good lighting, is unedited, and not screenshotted, with no major body parts cropped out. - If you wish to add text (ie. name, date, quote) to your illustration, please type this in the 'special instructions' field at checkout along with the font (1,2 or 3). - If you have any particular colour preferences, please also type this into the "special instructions" field at checkout. - Up to three changes are allowed to be made to ensure you are happy with the final illustration.

Your final order will include high quality PNG files sent to your email with three background options ( a white background; a colour I think compliments your illustration + an arch design). If you have a specific background colour you would like please state this in the 'special instructions' field at checkout.

Pricing is based on the photo provided ~ if you require major changes to the photo please email artby.tayah@hotmail.com for a quote.

If you have purchased a physical print, you will be able to choose which copy of the illustration you would like printed + shipped.

*frame not included

Please note ~ physical prints are only available Australia wide. If you are an international customer, please select the digital file option.
